Admissions

Join the Riara School of Computing Sciences by following these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (certificates, transcripts, etc.).
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend an admission interview (if required).
  5. Receive your admission letter.
